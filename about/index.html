About me

My name is Stefan Oderbolz ("Odi") and I live in Zurich.

I'm currently working for Liip as a web developer. I am one of the creators of Kort, an OpenStreetMap game.

I'm interested in Open Source software and the Open Data movement.

In my spare time, I play badminton and all kinds of tabletop games. I'm a film and TV series junkie, but I'm a keen reader, too.
